✏️ The Sketchbook page

A Sketchbook page is where you share the early stages of your creative process. It’s a place for your rough sketches, wireframes, brainstorming notes, and conceptual designs—ideas that haven’t been polished yet but are essential to understanding your thought process.

This page shows how you think, problem-solve, and approach design challenges.

Clients and collaborators will appreciate seeing the evolution of your ideas and the steps you take to refine and perfect them.

Use the Sketchbook page to highlight your creative journey and demonstrate your ability to turn concepts into tangible design solutions.
