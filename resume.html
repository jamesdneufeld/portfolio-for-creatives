Why Include a Resume?

Highlight Your Achievements

Your resume is an essential part of your portfolio—it helps potential employers, clients, and collaborators understand your qualifications and what you bring to the table. Including a resume on your website can make you stand out and present yourself professionally, even before you meet someone in person.

Think of it as a summary of your journey: the skills you’ve developed, the projects you’ve worked on, and the experiences that make you unique. You can link to your full resume, or even display it directly on your site to give a quick overview of your career highlights.
